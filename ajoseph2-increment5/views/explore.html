Mission Statement

MonoMuse is dedicated to giving a single artist the space they deserve. Each season, we curate one focused exhibition explored in depth.

We believe that truly understanding an artist requires immersion. By devoting our entire space to one voice at a time, MonoMuse creates an environment where visitors can move slowly and look closely. Our model means every visit is a new experience.

Milestones

  • 2016: MonoMuse opens its doors with its first single-artist exhibition.
  • 2018: MonoMuse receives an award for innovation.
  • 2023: MonoMuse expands its gallery space to accommodate larger immersive installations.
  • 2026: MonoMuse presents a Basquiat retrospective.
